# Carbon Emission Factors

## Data Sources

All emission factors in this application are sourced from internationally recognized, peer-reviewed databases and government agencies.

### Transport Emission Factors
- **EPA (U.S. Environmental Protection Agency)** - Vehicle emission standards
- **DEFRA (UK Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs)** - Transport emission factors
- **IPCC (Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change)** - International standards

### Food Emission Factors
- **Our World in Data** - Comprehensive food carbon footprint database (2024 data)
- **EPD (Environmental Product Declaration)** - Certified product declarations
- **Poore & Nemecek (2018)** - Published scientific database

### Energy Emission Factors
- **EPA eGRID** - U.S. electricity grid emissions
- **IEA (International Energy Agency)** - Global energy statistics
- **IPCC** - Energy sector emissions

### Shopping Emission Factors
- **Ecoinvent** - Life cycle assessment database
- **Carbon Trust** - Product carbon footprints
- **Company Environmental Reports** (Apple, etc.)

---

## Regular Updates

These factors should be updated annually to reflect changes in:
- Energy grid composition (renewable energy adoption)
- Manufacturing improvements
- Agricultural practices
- Transportation technology

---

## Disclaimer

Emission factors are **global averages** and may vary by:
- Geographic location
- Production methods
- Transportation distances
- Local energy grid composition

For enterprise applications, consider using region-specific factors.
