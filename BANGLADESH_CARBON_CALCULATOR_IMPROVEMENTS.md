## ‚ö° **3. Energy & Electricity Enhancements**

### Current State
- ‚úÖ Has: Electricity grid, Natural gas
- ‚ùå Missing: Bangladesh-specific electricity grid data

### Suggested Improvements:

#### **Bangladesh Electricity Grid**
- **Bangladesh Grid Average**: ~0.65 kg CO2/kWh
  - (Higher than global average due to fossil fuel dependency)
- **Solar Power**: 0 kg CO2/kWh (growing in Bangladesh)
- **Biomass**: ~0.1 kg CO2/kWh
- **Diesel Generator**: ~0.8 kg CO2/kWh (common backup)

#### **Common Appliances in Bangladesh**
- **Ceiling Fan** - Very common (most households have multiple)
  - Standard fan: ~0.05 kWh/hour
  - Energy-efficient fan: ~0.03 kWh/hour

- **AC (Split)** - Growing in middle/upper class
  - 1 ton AC: ~1.2 kWh/hour
  - 1.5 ton AC: ~1.8 kWh/hour
  - 2 ton AC: ~2.4 kWh/hour

- **Refrigerator** - Common
  - Small (150L): ~0.5 kWh/day
  - Medium (250L): ~0.8 kWh/day
  - Large (350L+): ~1.2 kWh/day

- **LED Bulb** - Replacing CFL
  - 7W LED: ~0.007 kWh/hour
  - 12W LED: ~0.012 kWh/hour

- **CFL Bulb** - Still common
  - 15W CFL: ~0.015 kWh/hour

- **TV** - Very common
  - LED TV (32"): ~0.05 kWh/hour
  - LED TV (42"+): ~0.08 kWh/hour

- **Washing Machine** - Growing usage
  - Semi-automatic: ~0.3 kWh/load
  - Fully automatic: ~0.5 kWh/load

- **Water Pump** - Common in buildings
  - Submersible pump: ~0.8 kWh/hour
  - Surface pump: ~0.6 kWh/hour

- **Electric Iron** - Common
  - Standard iron: ~1.0 kWh/hour

- **Rice Cooker** - Common
  - Standard: ~0.4 kWh/use

- **Electric Kettle** - Growing
  - Standard: ~0.15 kWh/use

#### **Seasonal Usage Patterns**
- **Summer (March-June)**: High fan/AC usage
- **Monsoon (July-September)**: Moderate usage
- **Winter (November-February)**: Lower usage, some heating
